## Backcasting

Backcasting is a normative approach of conducting a future study with the goal of designing a desirable future and formulating realistic steps to achieve this future.
Typical use cases of backcasting approaches can be found in city planning or sustainability goals.


## Workshop?

As a mean of collecting input about (1) the desirability of the designed future and (2) the steps needed to achieve it, a workshop has been carried out.
Five students in Bachelor and Master programs in the field of Information systems completed the workshop, offering opinions and thoughts on the matter.
