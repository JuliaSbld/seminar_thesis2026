## Technology Usage

The interaction with screen-based mobile devices e.g. smartphones is a dominant aspect of todays technology usage.
While these technologies enable many new ways of interacting with other humans and information, scholars have a high consensus about negative impacts of the technology (over-)usage many children and adults present.
In this thesis I will use the taxonomy of (Montag et al.) to clearly express the behavior discussed.
Here, Internet use disorder (IUD), which is an umbrella term over classified disorders enabled by digital devices, is split into predominantly mobile IUD and predominantly non-mobile IUD.

Scholars presented a multitude of negative effects, stemming from the Internet use disorder.
These include alterations of brain matter, suggesting higher rates in early stage dementia from excessive screen exposure. (Eikelboom, Neophytou)
Further, lowered self-esteem, increases in mental health issues and addiction as well as slower learning and acquisition are suggested in adolescents and young adults. (Neophytou)
The finding of increases in addiction is supported, when taking into account that videogame playing is associated with similar magnitude of dopamine release as drug usage. (Weinstein et al.)

## Lorm Alphabet

The deaf-blind austrian poet and philosophical writer, Hieronymus Lorm, has invented the lorm alphabet in 18xx.
It uses touch to convey the letters of the latin alphabet.
For that the hand of usually a deaf-blind person is touched by a communication partner.
Gestures like tapping and stroking different areas of the hand correspond to different letters, resulting in building sentences letter by letter.(Schmidt et al.) Fig.1
This is a relevant difference to sign language used by deaf individuals, which is not a letter by letter translation of the spoken language.
Therefore, the lorm alphabet can be viewed closer to a mean of input than its own language.
