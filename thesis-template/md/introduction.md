FF - How does a desirable future of audio based technology interaction look like and how do we get there?

A backcasting approach has been selected to systematically answer the proposed research question.
Therefore, a desirable future is proposed alongside the steps that are needed to achieve said future.
To enrich both parts of results, a student workshop has been carried out with the goal of gaining outside perspectives on the desirability of the proposed future as well as the steps needed to reach it.

The future scenario entails the widespread use of a new mobile technology consisting of an audio output and input piece (e.g. earbud or cochlear implant) and touch sensors offering the possibility of text input via the users hand using the lorm alphabet.
This audio and sensory interaction based technology is a desirable alternative to todays mainly visual mobile devices, because of its more focused approach to support the everyday life of its users.
